About Us

Rophe Community Services is a Non-Governmental Organization that provides health care services to people using drugs, HTS (HIV counseling and testing services), wellness programs and linkage to care to the community.

Our Services

Primary Health Care Clinic Services

  • Treatment for chronic conditions
  • Family planning services
  • Mother and child services
  • Antenatal Clinic
  • Drug rehabilitation services

Academic Services

  • Social Auxiliary level 5
  • Child and youth care level 5
  • Chemical Laboratory Analysis
  • FETC counseling course
  • Community health care courses
  • Short skills program
